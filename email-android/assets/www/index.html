Homescreen - first visit
Hello, looks like you're here for the first time! How about listening the tutorial and start configuring your account? Swipe to the left to continue with the tutorial!
This is your first tutorial page. This is the page where you can start to hear the tutorial of the application. Swipe left to continue with the tutorial.
Tutorial page 1
Good job! To navigate through the screens, just swipe left or right. Your phone will read for you the current screen title. For example, you can swipe right to go to the previous screen, or just swipe left to continue the tutorial.
This is your second tutorial page. This is the page where you continue to hear the tutorial of the application. Swipe right to listen again the previous part of the tutorial or swipe left to continue with the tutorial.
Tutorial page 2
Getting there! Some screens rely on other gestures like tap, long tap, or swipe up or down. You can try some gestures in this screen and your phone will try to read them. Available gestures are: tap, long tap, swipe up or swipe down. When you are done, swipe left to listen the last tutorial page.
This is your third tutorial page. This is the page where you can find the gestures you can do to navigate through pages, emails or contacts. Swipe right to listen again the previous part of the tutorial or swipe left to hear the last page of the tutorial.
Tutorial page 3
Almost done! You have to connect your email in order to get the max out of Echoes. You'll be able to do this on the next screen. For visually impaired or people with low vision, make sure you are visually assisted. Swipe left to connect your account.
This is your last tutorial page. Swipe right to listen again the previous part of the tutorial or swipe left to synchronize your email account.
Connect your email
Enter your email credentials, then swipe left to connect Echoes to your email.
